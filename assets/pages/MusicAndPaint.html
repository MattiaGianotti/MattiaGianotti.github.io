Music & Paint

Music & Paint is an innovative evolution of the Magic Room project focused on generative content. The first objectve of the project is aims to foster collaboration between people with moderate to severe learning disabilities and complex needs, and in particular autism. Secondly, our aim was to offer caregivers an innovative possible tool during their daily activities. It si known from litterature that painting and music therapy approaches can help to foster social interaction for autistic people. However, the tools sometimes lack of flexibility and fail to keep people's attention. Unknowns also remain about the effect of combining these approaches.

For this project we created an initial prototype usign the Magic Room tools, then we created a portable version of the smart space to bring the tool in contexts that could not create a dedicated room.

Timeframe of the project: 2023 - 2024

HOW DOES IT WORK?
The Experience

In our applicatin we used a shared frontal projection as a digital canvas for two users playing simultaneously. Each user has a handheld controller that allows them to paint, errase or move around the canvas to play the different melodies. Users can ineract with the system by clicking on the Internet of Thins (IoT) powered contoller or moving around the space. Players can share their melodies and drawings, either by combinig their drawings or playing eachothers melodies.

The system also include a hint system to support the users in case of need. It silently detect the different users behaviours searching for repetive patters of actions or inctivities and propose visual and auditory stimuly to suggest the user different actions, inspire a new interaction or attract the attention to the other player's drawing. Finally the caregiver can use the tablet to control the experience and help the users clean up the drawing, changing drawing color for the users, and force the hint system to triger a certain action.

Further detail on the design process can be found in this scientific paper.

The Generation syntax

Each visual has a music counterpart and conversely in order to provide organization, strucure, predicibility and armony to the final outcome; in this way the user can use its creativity to create whatever he desired predicting the outcome of its actions (fundamental for autistic people).

The experience relies on three layers:

  • Sound nodes are represened as paint spots
  • Melodies are represented as lines eventually passing over the paint spots
  • composition rules differentate between temporary melodies (create by moving fast on the canvas), normal melodies(created by moving slowly), or loops (melodied hat forms a close shape)
