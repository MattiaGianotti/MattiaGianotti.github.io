My Skills

Defining one's skills is a delicate exercise, a balancing act between overestimation and underestimation of one's abilities. I believe my skills lie at the intersection of creativity, technology, and problem-solving. These skills have been honed through years of active participation, management, and organization of projects involving multidisciplinary teams and an incredibly diverse range of professionals. Working alongside such talented individuals has been a profound learning experience, enriching my expertise and broadening my perspective. I am grateful for these experiences and remain eager to grow further, embracing every opportunity to learn and refine my capabilities. Below, I have curated a list of some of my professional skills to provide a clearer picture of my expertise.

Work related skills

Teamwork

I've had the privilege of collaborating with talented developers, brilliant designers just a few desks away, incredible psychologists, and many more amazing professionals. These experiences have allowed me to work cohesively in teams of various sizes—ranging from small groups on coding projects to multidisciplinary teams of over 20 professionals for large-scale, impactful research initiatives.

UX research

I come from an engineering background, but during my joint Ph.D. with the School of Design, I gained valuable experience in both qualitative and quantitative UX research. My work has included engaging with some of the most challenging user groups, such as individuals with neurodevelopmental disorders, which has deepened my understanding of user-centered design. I am highly confident in my Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) expertise and gamification UX skills, and I am eager to apply them to new fields and challenges.

Leadership and management

I began my journey as a programmer, gradually building extensive experience in design, management, and cutting-edge technological knowledge, particularly in the realm of innovative Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) techniques. My goal has always been to learn more about the field and to share that journey with others. I believe that leadership is about empowering the people around you to express their talents and grow together as a team.

Software and Hardware Architecture

Over the years, I have tackled the design of highly complex software architectures. I've learned a great deal from my mistakes and continuously improved my outcomes. Additionally, I've contributed to the theoretical development of modeling languages and frameworks to help organize my knowledge and enable others to avoid making the same mistakes.

Unity Game Development

For the majority of my career, and even before, I have used Unity Engine to create interactive experiences. I have developed over 20 games for research and university projects. Additionally, I have gained substantial expertise in editor scripting within Unity and have guided several others in learning how to use the platform effectively.

Software Development

I am a dedicated software developer. Over the years, I have developed a wide range of software using various programming languages and frameworks, always choosing the best tools for each specific project and time frame. I enjoy learning new skills and am excited to embrace new challenges!

Internet of Things

IThe Internet of Things (IoT) is a complex and fascinating realm, where it can mean 100 different things to 100 different users and developers, depending on the specific case. Over the years, I have worked to push Human-Centered IoT to its limits—and even beyond—learning both its potential and its pitfalls. Through trial and error, I have gained a unique perspective on this ever-evolving field.

Augmented, Virtual and Cross Reality

Throughout my career, I have worked across the spectrum of virtuality, with a particular focus on augmented reality. I have even explored unconventional technologies to push the boundaries of AR, as seen in the Magic Room projects. However, I have never overlooked the potential of virtual reality. Together with my colleagues, we pioneered true cross-reality experiences, as demonstrated in the 5A project.

Conversational Agents

I had the immense fortune of working in the field of chatbots and AI during one of the most transformative periods in the industry, marked by the rise of technologies like ChatGPT and similar advancements. I've had the unique opportunity to witness this evolution firsthand, gaining insights into how to harness its capabilities while also identifying its limitations.
