A bit of me

I bring with me a passion for all kinds of game-related activities, a logical and organizational mindset, and extensive experience in creating the most suitable experiences for even the most demanding users. My work spans multidisciplinary teams and involves Virtual and Augmented Reality, Conversational Agents, the Internet of Things, Distributed Systems, and Multi-Sensory experiences. My focus has always been on improving aid and support systems for the most vulnerable among us. I design for complex scenarios, relying on the latest scientific knowledge to create robust, reusable, and modular implementations, maximizing maintainability and enabling agile programming development.

Creativity and lateral thinking are integral to my approach, inspiring me and my colleagues to continuously challenge ourselves and strive for excellence.

I envision my future employer providing a thriving environment where I can grow, learn, collaborate, and have fun.
